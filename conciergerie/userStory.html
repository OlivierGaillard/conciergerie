Workhour

Concierge Usage

The concierge, which know almost nothing of a computer, will have to enter his working hours. So he enters the following data:

  1. date
  2. title
  3. duration in decimal format: 1.5 is 1h30min

Admin Usage

The manager diplay the list of all workhours and can filter by month, entering month and year. At the bottom of the list she can see the total of hours and the amount to pay. She can print this report.

She can set the amount of money per hour

Implementation

Enter hours

Nothing special here.

Generate salary sheet

We have to filter per month and year (one field) and to generate the report by summing the durations multiplied by the work-hour amount.

Filter the hour listing

We can simply use the package django-filter. It seems stable and up-to-date.
